Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the begining of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clikcing on each crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. Each time you click a crystal your total will tally up at the bottom, leaving you to determine the value of each crystal, and which one to click next to equal the magic number.

You win the game by matching your total score to the magic number. If you go over the magic number you lose!
