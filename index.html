Crystal Collector

You will be given a random number at the begining of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clikcing on each crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. If you go over the random number you lose!
